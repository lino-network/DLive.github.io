Position: Software Engineer

Description of duties:

Responsible for developing infrastructure and solutions for blockchain infrastructure, conducting data analysis and research, providing recommendations for product development. Specifically, the job duties include:

1. Analyze different video streaming infrastructure technologies. Dig into the technology details and present solutions for the company’s video streaming infrastructure.
2. Develop a live and video on demand infrastructure network. Test the bottleneck of infrastructure and improve the availability and scalability of this infrastructure.
3. Design and implement video platform and blockchain integration logic, which can deploy and maintain the information on blockchain by different validators.
4. Design and implement blockchain application based on the infrastructure, which can allow users to interact with the blockchain..

Requirements: Master’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or related plus 1 year experience or related.
